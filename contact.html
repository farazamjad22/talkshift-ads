Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable the keyboard?

Go to Settings > General > Keyboard > Add New Keyboard... and select TalkShift. Don’t forget to allow Full Access.

Why isn’t translation working?

Ensure you have an internet connection and both input/output languages are selected in the app settings.

Why is the clipboard translation not appearing?

Make sure you’ve copied some text and tapped the clipboard icon on the TalkShift keyboard.
