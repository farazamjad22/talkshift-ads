Privacy Policy for TalkShift

Effective Date: [26-05-2025]

1. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • The custom keyboard does not log keystrokes or capture anything you type.
  • We do not track your location, contacts, or any identifiable information.

2. How Translations Work

  • When you request a translation, the text is sent securely to the DeepL API.
  • The communication is encrypted and used only to return translated results.
  • We do not store your translation history or submitted text.

3. Clipboard Access

  • Clipboard content is only accessed when you tap the "clipboard translate" button.
  • We do not monitor your clipboard passively or in the background.

4. Third-Party Services

  • We use DeepL for translation. Please refer to their Privacy Policy for more information.

5. Data Shared with Apple

  • We comply with Apple’s App Store guidelines. No user data is shared with Apple outside what is required by iOS system services.
